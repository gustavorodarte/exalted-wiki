**Name**: Urza Aolan

Titles: 

Type: No moon

Faction: Winding Path

**Description**: Urza is a small woman, 1.5 meters tall and weighing 40 kg. She has the elegant and delicate bearing of a ballerina. Her hair is silver and her skin is white and pale, resembling porcelain, **she has bright blue eyes**. She has a fragile and delicate appearance, and does not appear to be more than 16 years old.

**Tell: **Her skin is slightly cooler and has a slight blue undertone.

**Motivation**: Protecting Creation from Demonic Influence

**Intimacy:** (Emotion) **Hate:** Deeply despises demons, their cults, and the corrupting influence they exert. 

(Emotion) **Love:** Cherishes her new family, including her husband Scylla and their children. 

(Belief) **Critical Thinking:** Believes that one must think critically about the world; blind devotion and uncontrolled emotions can obscure a person’s true calling. 

(Emotion) **Solar Bound:**  From the moment she first laid eyes on Ravi, she knew it, she felt a profound and enduring bond, an overwhelming sense of Wholeness rooting her to her Solar Mate in a way that transcends words, as if it was always meant to be, as pure and steady as the unconditional love for a child.
